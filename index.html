About Us

Swami Lilashah Masala Centre located at Amir Road Area of Palanpur, GUJARAT is the grand hub selling all the types of Spices and Condiments which are manufactured in our own unit. All the Spices and Condiments are brought-out for sale after their high quality check which assures orignailty and purity of the every product for all the kitchens across India.

From around 50 Years of the trust of all the worthy customers...we stand strong for selling gauranteed and exceptionally remarkable quality of all the vivid range of our products which satisfies demand of every stakeholder.

Swami Masala Offers non-adultered and sorted quality of Organic Spices which are healthful as well as everytime adding unforgettable taste to your dailylife food and also bringing mesmerizing sensations to the taste cells of every individual.

Available Products (Both in Raw-Dried and Powdered form) :- Red Chillies(Lal-Mirch) ,Turmeric(Haldi), Coriander(Dhaana),Cumin(Jeera) ,Mustard seeds(Raai),Carom seeds(Ajwain), Fennel(Saunf), Black and White Pepper(Kaali and Safed Mari), Cinnamon(Dalchini),Green & Black Cardamom (Hari and Kaali Elaichi), etc...along with each and every variety of all the other condiments also.
